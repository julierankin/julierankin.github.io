Public Services Librarian, Job Description

The Public Services Librarian oversees reference, instruction and access services for Oklahoma Baptist University Library patrons.

Overall Tasks

Reference

Manages student workers in the public services department. Trains student workers in circulation and reference procedures. Provides references services to students, faculty, staff and guests.

Instruction

Provides information literacy instruction (in person and remote) for students throughout the stages of their college career. Conducts student learning/library use assessments. Collaborate with faculty and staff to develop appropriate information literacy instruction for all students. Create and update learning objects such as online tutorials and research guides.

Access

Assists and participates in website maintenence. Manages and maintains the reserve materials. Assures the circulation of ILL materials. Participates in collection development responsibilities.

Outreach

Manages library programming and outreach. Advocate for user needs regarding the library's systems, tools and technology. Manage the library's social media presence.
